Transformation
Accelerate your network transformation
Network transformation is about much more than just technology. It requires a nuanced approach with experts who take a broad view of your business strategy and goals and translate them into network outcomes your customers expect.
You need an experienced, trusted partner
Are there a few trouble spots in your network or are you thinking of a comprehensive network transformation? We collaborate with you leveraging your business insights and our industry experience. Our team combines data analytics and tailored automation tools with our proven methodology to ensure an effective network transformation that meets your business needs and maximizes ROI.
Network transformation in action
Sometimes the hardest part about transforming your network is getting started. Take a look at these resources to learn how we can help you overcome barriers and identify opportunities to maximize ROI, applying our experience and expertise to deliver successful outcomes—even when we’re not part of your network.
Network transformation here and now
Your network must support high-quality bandwidth and accommodate steadily increasing, unpredictable traffic. Legacy networks can’t hold up. This eBook explores the barriers that stifle network transformation and outlines a well-proven methodology for project success.
Verizon and Ciena collaborate to deliver successful network transformation
Verizon needed to transform their network and turned to an experienced partner for assistance. Verizon’s journey of modernization enables the delivery of new services and increases network reliability and operating efficiency, providing the roadmap for digital transformation for its customers.
Taking network transformation efficiency to the next level
Business models are being rebuilt from the ground up and transforming networks has become a mission critical task. Ciena Services helps maximize efficiency and ROI relying on a proven analytical approach focused on business outcomes.
Upgrade fixed-grid network to next-gen optical network flexibility.
Maximizing efficiency and business outcomes for a cloud leader.
Migrate complex legacy platforms onto a next-generation network.
Reducing risk and OPEX for a Tier-1 communications service provider.
Services-led multi-vendor transformation for a non-Ciena network.
Dramatically faster planning and migration, and a permanent improvement in operational processes.
Ciena’s transformation methodology
Sometimes it can be tempting to jump into a transformation project and just start deploying equipment and migrating circuits. Our experience has shown that spending sufficient time on strategy and planning upfront leads to successful outcomes.
Ciena has a proven, comprehensive methodology for network transformation that we’ve used with customers around the world.
